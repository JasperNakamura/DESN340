Pencil No.2

Think with your hands.

Surface Pressure

Expressive from every angle. Pencil No.2's unique tip is shaped to create lines of all sizes without any settings. Use the point for fine details and the angled edge for broad strokes.

Simply Works

When you need to get something down fast, Pencil is ready.

No Battery Needed

Under normal use, you won't need to recharge Pencil No.2 forever. It has no internal battery and can keep working so long as the tip is shaven down with a pencil sharpener.

Kiss to Pair ™

Bluetooth connection as it should be: no settings menus, no confusing pairing rituals. Just press Pencil's tip to the screen. Clear, stable, simple... easy as falling in love.

Plays nicely

Palm Rejectin, Erase, and Blend work only on Paper and other supported applications, but Pencil also works as a non-connected stylus in any other app and on any touchscreen device.
