Junji Ito

Japanese horror manga artist. Some of his most notable works include Tomie, a series chronicling an immortal girl who drives her stricken admirers to madness;
Uzumaki, a three-volume series about a town obsessed with spirals; and Gyo, a two-volume story in which fish are controlled by a strain of sentient bacteria called "the death stench."
Ito's work has developed a substantial cult following, with some deeming him a significant figure in recent horror iconography.
